Our Solutions

Electric Works, Inc. can handle all your residential electrical service needs. Our licensed electricians will assess the requirements, provide a written estimate, and complete the work to our exacting standards.

  • Electrical panel upgrades
  • Replacement of defective panels
  • Upgrades on switches, outlets
  • Tamper resistant child safety receptacles
  • Smoke/carbon monoxide alarms
  • Whole-house surge protection
  • Ceiling fan installation
  • Complete home high-efficiency light bulb installation
